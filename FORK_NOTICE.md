repository.

## Why "Xenomorphic"?

Like the Xenomorph, Zed is the incubation host. Xenomorphic bursts out
of its chest to evolve into something new — and inhuman.

This is not "a fork of Zed with some changes." This is a predator that
outgrew the host. The goal is to strip the organism down to its skeletal
advantages (GPUI, DeltaDB, a world-class editor core) and rebuild it
as something fundamentally different: **the control plane for posthuman
programming** — a modern, high-performance `tmux` deeply integrated with
an editor and the most technically advanced agent ever built.

Upstream improvements to fundamentals like DeltaDB may be merged as
patches in the future, but that's not guaranteed. The divergence is
intentional and accelerating.
